Youth and Student Department (YSD)

The Youth and Student Department, also known as YSD, plays a vital role as an integral part of the larger Youth and Students for Peace (YSP) organization, the YSD is responsible for nurturing and guiding the young people and second-generation members within the Unification Church community.

The YSD's primary mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment for young people to grow and develop their faith, as well as to foster a strong sense of camaraderie and connection among members. This department aims to instill in young members a deep understanding and appreciation for the teachings and traditions of the Unification Church, and to help them cultivate a personal relationship with True Parents

In order to achieve its goals, the YSD provides a wide range of programs, activities, and resources to young people and second-generation members. These include educational classes, leadership development workshops, community service opportunities, and cultural events. Through these initiatives, the YSD seeks to equip young members with the tools they need to become responsible and engaged leaders within their communities and beyond.

Overall, the Youth and Student Department is a crucial component of the Unification Church community, serving as a hub of support and guidance for the next generation of believers. Through its tireless efforts, the YSD helps to ensure that the values and principles of the Unification Church are passed down from one generation to the next, ensuring the continued growth and prosperity of the faith.
